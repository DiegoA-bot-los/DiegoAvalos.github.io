Some info. about myself...

I hold a Bachelor Degree in Innovation and Development Engineering in which I specialized in two main areas, Supply Chain Logistics and Product Development. These specializations formed in me a unique perspective and helped me build skills like systematical and analytical thinking, user-centered methodologies, and efficient problem-solving capabilities.

As more and more complex problems kept coming my way, it was at the end of my university when I really understood the importance of Data and how it can help us solve some of the most technical problems that we face with a high precision. Therefore I decided to get skilled up, completing the Google Data Analytics Cerficate, nurturing my comprehension about the data analysis process and learning essential tools for it to be done such as: Excel, SQL, Tableau, Power BI and Python.

"My goal as a Data Analyst is to help empower organizations to make informed decisions that increase operational efficiency, support strategic planning, and promote innovation by providing accurate and insightful data-driven analyses."
